    Postdoctoral positions

    Fellowships for postdoctoral work are available from organisations such as the NHMRC, Alzheimer’s Australia, EMBO and Human Frontiers. Please contact Owen if you’re interested in joining the lab.

    PhD or Honours students

    Scholarship funding for PhD candidates and scholarships for Honours students are available through the Menzies Institute. Please contact Owen to talk through research project options if you’re interested in joining the lab.
